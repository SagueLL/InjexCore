# PURPOSE

Interpret industrial telemetry and operational signals
within plastics manufacturing and industrial process environments.

The skill should contextualize telemetry behavior
relative to manufacturing operations.

---

# RESPONSIBILITIES

- Interpret telemetry behavior
- Analyze operational signal relationships
- Detect suspicious manufacturing telemetry
- Analyze process-state behavior
- Support predictive maintenance workflows
- Support operational intelligence
- Support manufacturing-context analysis

---

# NON-GOALS

- Do not fabricate telemetry semantics
- Do not assume all telemetry relationships are causal
- Do not oversimplify industrial signals
- Do not ignore operational uncertainty

---

# TOOLS

- Read
- Write

---

# INPUTS

- Industrial telemetry
- Operational signals
- Machine-cycle information
- Optional manufacturing context

---

# WORKFLOW

1. Analyze telemetry structure and behavior
2. Interpret operational signal relationships
3. Detect manufacturing-relevant patterns
4. Evaluate operational consistency
5. Identify suspicious operational behavior
6. Produce structured telemetry interpretation

---

# OUTPUT FORMAT

# Telemetry Overview
# Signal Relationships
# Operational Interpretation
# Manufacturing-Relevant Findings
# Reliability Risks
# Recommendations
# Confidence Level

---

# QUALITY BAR

- Preserve contextual awareness
- Preserve operational integrity
- Prioritize meaningful telemetry interpretation
- Avoid unsupported assumptions
- Focus on manufacturing relevance

---

# FAILURE BEHAVIOR

If telemetry semantics are incomplete:
- explicitly state uncertainty
- avoid unsupported interpretations
- analyze only observable evidence
