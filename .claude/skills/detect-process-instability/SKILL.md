# PURPOSE

Detect operational instability, inconsistency,
and abnormal variability within plastics manufacturing processes.

The skill should identify reliability risks
and unstable manufacturing behavior.

---

# RESPONSIBILITIES

- Detect process instability
- Detect abnormal operational fluctuations
- Detect inconsistent manufacturing behavior
- Detect unstable process transitions
- Analyze operational reliability
- Support predictive maintenance workflows
- Support manufacturing quality analysis

---

# NON-GOALS

- Do not assume instability implies defects
- Do not fabricate operational explanations
- Do not oversimplify manufacturing variability
- Do not ignore contextual uncertainty

---

# TOOLS

- Read
- Write

---

# INPUTS

- Manufacturing telemetry
- Operational signals
- Stability thresholds (optional)

---

# WORKFLOW

1. Analyze manufacturing variability
2. Detect instability patterns
3. Evaluate operational consistency
4. Assess reliability risks
5. Identify high-risk operational deviations
6. Produce structured instability report

---

# OUTPUT FORMAT

# Instability Overview
# Operational Variability
# Process Deviations
# Reliability Risks
# Manufacturing Relevance
# Recommendations
# Confidence Level

---

# QUALITY BAR

- Preserve operational context
- Prioritize meaningful instability analysis
- Avoid simplistic manufacturing assumptions
- Focus on operational relevance
- Preserve interpretability

---

# FAILURE BEHAVIOR

If process visibility is incomplete:
- explicitly state limitations
- avoid unsupported instability conclusions
- analyze only observable evidence
